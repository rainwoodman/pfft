section{Search and replace patterns}

Correct alignment of pfft.h header begin{lstlisting} %s/^( [^ ]+[^\]*) \/ 1\/g end{lstlisting}

Expand most macros of pfft.h to generate the function reference of this manual: begin{lstlisting}[language=bash,prebreak=textbackslash,] sed -e ‘s/ *\$//g’ -e ‘s/PFFT_EXTERN //g’

-e ‘s/PX(([^)]*))/pfft_1/g’ -e ‘s/ INT/ ptrdiff_t/g’ -e ‘s/ R/ double/g’ -e ‘s/ C/ pfft_complex/g’ -e ‘s/^ //g’ pfft.h > pfft.h.expanded

end{lstlisting}
